Privacy Policy

Effective date: April 2, 2026

Waketale ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the Waketale mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and display name.

Sleep data: We collect sleep check-in data you provide (bedtime, wake time, mood, sleep quality) to generate your personalized sleep program.

Usage data: Anonymous analytics data (screens visited, feature usage) collected via Firebase Analytics and Mixpanel to improve the app.

Subscription data: Purchase and subscription status managed by RevenueCat. We do not store payment card details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and personalize the AI CBT-I sleep coaching program
  • To generate sleep scores, action plans, and progress insights
  • To send bedtime and morning reminder notifications (if enabled)
  • To process and manage your subscription
  • To improve app features and fix bugs

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on Supabase (PostgreSQL) servers hosted on AWS. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. AI chat conversations are processed via Groq API and are not permanently stored beyond your session history.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with trusted service providers:

  • Supabase – database and authentication
  • Firebase / Google – analytics and crash reporting
  • Groq – AI language model for sleep coaching
  • RevenueCat – subscription management
  • Mixpanel – product analytics

5. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and all associated data at any time from the app's Settings → Delete Account screen. Upon deletion, your data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

6. Children's Privacy

Waketale is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via in-app notice.

8. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Email us at: privacy@waketale.com
